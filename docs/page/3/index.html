Who's Fernando?

I’m Fernando Paolo, a postdoctoral scholar @ NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Caltech. I develop algorithms for remote-sensing applications and large-scale data analysis, to study the Earth’s changing climate… Short bio I first became interested in polar research during my undergraduate years while participating in an expedition to Antarctica for over a month. Seeking a multidisciplinary Earth Science education I studied Oceanography at the University of Sao Paulo, Brazil, where I had the opportunity to participate in several scientific cruises in the South Atlantic Ocean....
